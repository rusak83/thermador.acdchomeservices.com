Thermador ice maker service details

We service column, French door, and undercounter Thermador ice makers. Diagnostics include checking temperature, water pressure, harvest cycles, and drain lines. OEM parts, written estimates, and workmanship warranty included. Book a visit and we’ll restore full production fast.

Typical problems

  • No ice production or very small batches
  • Ice tastes bad, smells musty, or appears cloudy
  • Frozen fill tubes or water line leaks
  • Bin sensors or harvest motors stuck
  • Grinding, chirping, or buzzing noises
  • Undercounter drain pan overflowing
  • Refrigerator door ice module not dispensing
  • Service/water filter warnings persist
