What drives Thermador pricing?

Freedom column sealed systems

Dual compressors, TFT controls, and stainless interiors take time to access. Clearing drain heaters or swapping evaporators often pushes the job toward the top of the range.

Pro Grand dual-fuel ranges

Ranges with ExtraLow burners, steam ovens, or griddles require calibration plus gas/electric testing, so labor is higher than a standard cooktop.

Zeolite + Home Connect components

Sapphire dishwashers and Masterpiece ovens use connected boards and zeolite drying cartridges. OEM parts keep them reliable but can add a few hundred dollars.

DIY vs pro?

Pulling a Freedom column or opening a Pro Grand steam cavity without the right tools risks cabinetry, flooring, and electronics. Let us handle the heavy work while you keep the kitchen showroom-ready.

Thermador Freedom column repair

Freedom columns blend into custom cabinetry with push-to-open doors, TFT displays, and dedicated compressors. We service refrigerator, freezer, and wine columns to keep temperatures steady and panels aligned.

Common column issues

  • Warm spots or high-temp alarms after a defrost cycle
  • Water pooling because the Cool Air Flow drain froze
  • Push-to-open actuators sticking or misaligned
  • Ice maker or water dispenser leaking behind panels
  • TFT display errors or Home Connect communication faults

Models we handle

  • T30IR / T30IF refrigerator & freezer columns
  • T24UW beverage/wine columns
  • Pre-configured refrigerator/freezer sets
  • Panel-ready & stainless installations

Pro Grand & Pro Harmony ranges

Thermador ranges combine ExtraLow burners, star-shaped flames, steam ovens, and interchangeable griddles. We keep every module calibrated so the range stays the centerpiece of the kitchen.

Service calls we see daily

  • ExtraLow burners clicking or dropping too low
  • Steam oven cavity leaking or showing E115/E150 errors
  • Griddle temperatures uneven or thermostat out of spec
  • Oven preheat taking too long / not reaching setpoint
  • SoftClose doors sagging on 48” and 60” models

Range lineup

  • PRG/PRD Pro Grand dual-fuel
  • PRL Pro Harmony gas ranges
  • 48” & 60” steam oven configurations
  • Rangetops with star burners & grills

Masterpiece wall & steam ovens

Masterpiece ovens, convection steam models, and Home Connect speed ovens need precise sensors and clean airflow. We swap blower motors, probes, hinges, and upgrade firmware so every bake is perfect.

Frequent oven issues

  • F34/F74 sensor errors or touchscreen lag
  • Steam oven lime scale or drain pump faults
  • Cooling fan noise vibrating through cabinetry
  • Door hinges failing to soft close
  • Wi-Fi/Home Connect setup not completing

Covered models

  • ME/MC Masterpiece single & double ovens
  • CS series convection steam ovens
  • Speed ovens & microwave drawers
  • Warming drawers & accessory modules
