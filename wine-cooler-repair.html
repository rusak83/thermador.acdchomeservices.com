Thermador wine cooler repair essentials

We service Thermador wine columns and undercounter wine coolers with single or dual zones. Our technicians stabilize temperature, humidity, and vibration to protect your collection.

Typical problems

  • Warm cabinet or unstable temperatures
  • Dual-zone not holding set points
  • Condensation, leaks, or excess humidity
  • Fans or compressor running constantly
  • Door seal issues or panel misalignment
  • Control board, sensor, or display errors
  • Vibration or unusual noise affecting bottles
