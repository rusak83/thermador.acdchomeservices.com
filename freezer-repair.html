Typical Thermador Freezer Issues

  • High-temp alarms or columns not reaching setpoint
  • Ice buildup on evaporators or drawers
  • Noise or vibration from condenser and evaporator fans
  • Ice makers overflowing or not producing cubes
  • Vacuum condenser light won’t clear after cleaning
  • Control board error codes after a power surge

We log fault codes and pressures for every visit so follow-ups are faster and your Thermador stays factory-calibrated.
